Our Story

A Legacy of Integrity & Professional Excellence

P.C. Bindal & Co. was established in 1983 to serve clients in audit and taxation. Over four decades, we have evolved into a comprehensive professional services firm with an uncompromising commitment to professional standards and ethics.

Headquartered in Jammu with branches at Delhi and Noida, serving clients across Pan-India with a team of 63 professionals.

We serve listed and non-listed companies spanning textiles, construction, power, retail, commodities, healthcare, insurance, hospitality, media, and IT sectors.

Our firm carries an image of uncompromising professional standards, having carved a significant position in India's audit and allied sector.

Founded by Padam Chandra Bindal, FCA, the firm continues its tradition of excellence under a distinguished team of 12 partner-level Chartered Accountants.
